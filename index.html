USA

The United States of America is a federal republic located primarily in North America. It is the world's third-largest country by total area and the
third-most populous. The country has a diverse population and is known for its cultural and economic influence on the world stage.

CHINA

China is the world's most populous country and the fourth-largest in terms of land area. It is located in East Asia and has a rich cultura
l heritage spanning over 5,000 years. China is known for its economic growth, technological advancements, and significant contributions to art, literature, and philosophy.

QATAR

Qatar is a sovereign country located in Western Asia, occupying the small Qatar Peninsula on the northeastern coast of the Arabian
Peninsula. It is known for its high-income economy, modern architecture, and being a major center for oil and natural gas production.
